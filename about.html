Our Story

Built for
Curious Minds.

MindStark is more than a library — it's a sanctuary for readers who believe that great books change lives.

Our Mission

Knowledge Should Be Free for Everyone

We started MindStark with a simple belief — that access to great books shouldn't be a privilege. Too many curious minds are locked out of the world's greatest ideas simply because of cost or geography.

MindStark tears down those walls. We bring together public domain classics, open-access titles, and curated modern works into one beautiful, easy-to-use platform — free for anyone with a curious mind.

Whether you're a student, a lifelong learner, a researcher, or just someone who loves getting lost in a good story — MindStark was built for you.

How We Got Here

The MindStark Story

2022

The Idea Is Born

Frustrated by paywalls and cluttered reading platforms, our founder set out to build the library he always wished existed — beautiful, free, and built for real readers.

2023

First 1,000 Books

MindStark launched its first catalog of 1,000 curated public domain titles. Within three months, over 500 readers had joined the platform.

2024

Membership & Growth

We introduced Reader Pro and Scholar membership plans, expanding the catalog to 10,000+ books and welcoming our 5,000th member.

2026

Today & Beyond

With 8,400+ active members across the globe, MindStark continues to grow — adding new books, features, and community tools every month.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we build at MindStark is guided by these principles.

📖

Open Access

We believe knowledge belongs to everyone. That's why we'll always offer a meaningful free tier — no credit card, no expiry, no strings attached.

✨

Beautiful Experience

Reading is a pleasure, and your reading platform should be too. We obsess over design, speed, and usability so you can focus on the words that matter.

🌍

Global Community

Books connect us across cultures, languages, and generations. MindStark is for every curious mind — wherever in the world they happen to be reading.

🔒

Privacy First

Your reading habits are personal. We never sell your data, never show ads, and never track you beyond what's needed to improve your experience.

🚀

Always Improving

MindStark is never "done." We ship updates constantly, listen closely to our community, and always ask — how can we make reading better?

💛

Reader-Centric

Every feature we build starts with one question: does this make reading more enjoyable? If the answer isn't a clear yes, we go back to the drawing board.
